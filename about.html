    About Us



    aConnect.io is a Amazon Consultancy Partner.  

    We provide professional services and fully managed services centred around Amazon Connect with expertise on the wider AWS platform and Technology landscape. We have a history that spans over 20 years within the Contact Centre discipline architecting, building and supporting contact centres from 100s to 1000s of agents and complex business logic for self-service applications and CRM integrations.   



    Dan Bloy

    Founder


    Dan is the Founder of aConnect.io, he has a passion and drive for technology that always burns bright. His first connection with Contact Centres was talking to Customers, this quickly turned into understanding business logic and routing, then progressed to Architecture, Build, Pre-sales support and Leadership. Working on Platforms from Cisco, Avaya, Microsoft, Aspect and now Amazon Connect.

We appreciate the complexity of the contact centre solution within businesses that spans People, Process, Technology and Customers, and that a migration activity has to be orchestrated and executed with support from the Technology and Business teams. We hold Certifications and Accreditations from Amazon for AWS and Amazon Connect.
